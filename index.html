welcome to the super Gym

A brand new 20,000 sq ft Super Gym is opening in Blanchardstown. This state of the art gym features the first heated Polar Chamber where you can track your heart rate and calories on screen. The gym also includes cutting edge Strength Equipment, Cardio Machines, Spin Studio, an Astro area and a Large Studio for Pilates and Yoga. Our Gyms are packed with the very latest, high quality, gym equipment that suits everyone to make sure you have the best work out and get the best results. Free parking for up to 80 cars. Buses that pass by are 37, 220a, 239. Coolmine train station is 15 minutes walk. Blanchardstown shopping centre is a 15 minute walk, or a short bus ride on the 37 or 39.
